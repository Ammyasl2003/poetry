About Author


GURSIMRAT KAUR
Gursimrat Kaur, born on July 24, 2009, in Chandigarh, is a dedicated student currently attending Shishu Niketan Public School-43 Chandigarh. She is a passionate writer and love reading. Her writing reflects her positive attitude, incorporating themes of humility, calmness, joy, and happiness. As a spiritual writer, she aims to spread positivity through her poems, thoughts, and articles. Gursimrat finds inspiration in listening to Gurbaani, exploring Punjabi literature, and learning Urdu. She hopes to inspire and connect with others through her work, aiming to leave a lasting legacy.
