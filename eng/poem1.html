A bittersweet reminder of happier days.
Your smile, a fleeting glimpse of the past,

A reminder of joy that cannot be regained.
My heart, a heavy burden bears,
Longing for the warmth of your loving care.
In dreams, I see your gentle face,
A comforting presence in a darkened place.
Though death has taken you far away,
Your memory remains, a love that never fades.
